ОТВЕТЫ:
1) A constructor is called at the time of declaration of an object. 
2) A constructor is called at the time of use of an object.

4) A constructor is called at the time of use of a class.

<br>
<br>
ПРАВИЛЬНЫЙ ОТВЕТ:
1) A constructor is called at the time of declaration of an object. 


<br> <br>
ОТВЕТ РАЗВЕРНУТЫЙ:
While creating an object, memory is allocated to that object when its
constructor is invoked, it invokes only once during declaration of object, if
you have not defined any constructor inside class then default constructor is
invoked.
